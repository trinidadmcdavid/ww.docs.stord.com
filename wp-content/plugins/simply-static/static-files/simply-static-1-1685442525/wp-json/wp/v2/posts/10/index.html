Mykhailo Podoliak, an adviser to Ukrainian President Volodymyr Zelensky, denied the drone strike in Moscow in response to a question on a television program. He said, ‘We are not involved in any way.’<\/p>\n\n\n\n

Moscow is more than 1000 kilometers away from Ukraine. Drone strikes in Moscow have been rare since the start of the Russia-Ukraine war. However, other parts of Russia are subject to almost regular drone strikes.<\/p>\n\n\n\n
